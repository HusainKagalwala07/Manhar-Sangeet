Manhar Sangeet Rachna (Part I / 2 nd Edition)

Book Name    Manhar Sangeet Rachna Part I
Author Pt.Murli Manohar Shukla
Compilation Dr.Shyamrang Shukla
Publisher Pt.Murli Manohar Shukla
Copyrights Shrimati Priti Hemant Heble
Edition 1stEdn.Vijayadashmi,22ndOctober 1985
2ndEdn.August 2015
Price Rs.350/-

Book Description

Manhar Sangeet Rachna (Part I / 2 nd Edition) includes Swar Sadhna Vidhi of Jaipur Kunwar Shyam Gharana, Music Theory, Raag Description, Taal Description, Traditions of Jaipur Kunwar Shyam Gharana, along with Pt. Murli Manohar Shukla’s 150 Compositions (Bandish, Drupad, Thumri, Tarana) belonging to 45 Raag set in 10 Taal with 4 Laya. Book also serves as reference to Beginner & Intermediate Students of Hindustani Classical Music opting for Visharad, Alankaar or equivalent exams.

Goswami Kunwar Shyam Gharana Swar Sadhna Vidhi

Book Name Goswami Kunwar Shyam Gharana Swar Sadhna Vidhi
Research &Enhanced Pt.Murli Manohar Shukla
Publisher Pt.Murli Manohar Shukla
Copyrights Shrimati Priti Hemant Heble
Edition 1stEdn.Vijayadashmi,21stNovember 2009
Price Rs.50/-

Book Description

Goswami Kunwar Shyaam Gharana Swar Sadhana Vidhi (1 st Edition) includes basic of Vocal development or Voice Culture excercises that help to enhance the knowledge of Swar alongside with Tonal Quality. A set of Alankaar’s or Patterns are developed so that the Gaayaki Ang can be developed not only for Voice but also to develop the Vocal Element on Instruments. Book is a reference to all from Beginners to Advance.
